Ms. J's Pecan Pie Recipe

Ms. J's favorite food is pecan pie. But how does she make it?

Let's take a look at one way to make a delicious pecan pie.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar (1/2 brown, 1/2 white)
  • 1 cup corn syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • About 1 cup pecan halves
  • 1 pie crust (homemade or storebought), unbaked

Directions

  1. Combine butter, sugar, and corn syrup in a medium saucepan
  2. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until sugar dissolves and butter is melted
  3. Cool slightly, then add eggs, vanilla, and salt
  4. Stir well, then pour into unbaked pie crust
  5. Arrange pecan halves on top
  6. Bake at 325 degrees for around 50-60 minutes, or until just set
  7. Let the pie cool completely so that it will set further. Then, enjoy!
